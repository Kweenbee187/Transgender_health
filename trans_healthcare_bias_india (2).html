Explanation Panel
🔴 What is Bias?

When a model uses only the group mean as prediction, individuals who deviate from the mean receive high errors. Low-access regions amplify this.

error_i = |actual_i − group_mean|
Bias = max(MAE) − min(MAE)
🟡 Access & Care Index (ACI)

Reflects healthcare infrastructure quality. Low ACI → high noise → larger prediction errors → greater disparity.

High: Delhi · Maharashtra · Karnataka · Gujarat · Punjab
Med: Tamil Nadu · Kerala · W.Bengal · M.Pradesh · Telangana
Low: Bihar · Assam · Rajasthan · U.Pradesh · Odisha · Jharkhand
🟢 Fairness Mitigation

Fairness-aware model gives higher weight to poorly-predicted individuals, reducing MAE spread across states.

w_i = 1 / (error_i + ε)
fair_pred = Σ(w·actual) / Σ(w)
